💡 How to read this page: Two visual approaches tell the multi-project story at a glance.
  • Bubble + Heatmap (default): Bubble chart = fiscal/water/jobs tradeoff in one view. Heatmap = color-coded severity across all metrics.
  • Risk Scorecard: Each project × community row gets colored risk tiles per category — scannable, no math required.
  • Community Dimension: Use the View by… bar to pivot between “projects only” and per-community breakdowns — see how a golf course affects Gorham differently than Westbrook.
