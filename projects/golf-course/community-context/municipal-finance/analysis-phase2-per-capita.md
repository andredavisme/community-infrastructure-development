## Key Observations

- **North Berwick** BETE per capita ($49,466) is the highest in the county — its industrial tax base (Pratt & Whitney) serves a small residential population of ~5,000.
- **Homestead per capita** is remarkably uniform ($3,500–$7,000), reflecting consistent residential character across the county.
- **Dayton** ($6,961 HS/capita) and **Berwick** ($6,770 HS/capita) lead homestead intensity — high residential ownership relative to population.
- Large urban centers (Biddeford, Sanford, Saco) show moderate per capita figures despite leading in absolute totals — population dilutes the ratio.
- **Ogunquit's** elevated PP per capita ($6,975) reflects its concentrated hospitality/commercial sector within a very small residential population.
