# Community Context

Demographic, geographic, and fiscal data for the host community and nearby communities affected by or relevant to the development scenario.

## Host Community Profile

Data tracked for the primary host municipality:

- Population (current + 25-year trend)
- Age distribution and household composition
- Median household income
- Employment base and major employers
- Municipal tax revenue (business, property, excise)
- Municipal budgeted expenses by category
- Existing infrastructure capacity (water, sewer, roads)

## Nearby Community Profile

Same dimensions tracked for communities within a defined radius (e.g., 10–20 miles) that may:
- Supply labor to the development
- Be affected by water or environmental impacts
- Draw visitors or revenue from the development
- Share regional infrastructure or services

## Historical Data Range

Where available, all community context data is collected across a **25-year window (2000–2025)** to establish:

- Baseline conditions before modeled development
- Long-term population and fiscal trends
- Economic cycles and their effect on municipal revenue
- Prior development impacts for comparison

## Folders

- `population/` — Census and estimate data, trends, demographics
- `municipal-finance/` — Tax income, budgeted expenses, per-capita analysis
- `nearby-communities/` — Profiles and data for regional communities
- `trend-analysis/` — 25-year summaries and visualizations
