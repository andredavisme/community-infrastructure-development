## TIF / Exemption Risk

- Rating: **Low**
- Golf courses rarely qualify for BETR, TIF, or LIHTC incentive programs in Maine
- No abatement expected; full assessed value taxable from completion

## Fiscal Notes

- School cost risk: **Low** — no new residents generated
- Emergency services: minimal incremental demand (seasonal, daytime)
- Road impact: moderate seasonal traffic increase on access roads
- Net fiscal position: likely positive (revenue >> services cost) for host municipality
