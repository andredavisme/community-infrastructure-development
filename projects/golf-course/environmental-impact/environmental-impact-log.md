## Chemical Use Profile

| Input | Application Rate | Season | Risk Level | Mitigation |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Herbicides (pre/post emergent) | ~2–4 lb ai/ac/yr on treated areas | Apr–Oct | Medium | IPM program; buffer zones |
