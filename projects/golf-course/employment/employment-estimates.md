## Job Quality Notes

- Permanent jobs are predominantly seasonal (Apr–Oct) with limited year-round employment
- Wage tiers: entry (~$15–18/hr), mid (~$22–30/hr), skilled ($35–55/hr), management ($65K–90K salary)
- High likelihood of local/regional hire for grounds and F&B roles
- Management roles may require national recruitment
