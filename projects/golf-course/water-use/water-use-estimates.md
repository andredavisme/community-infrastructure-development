## Risk Notes

- Summer peak demand (~312,000 gpd) coincides with community peak demand (Jul–Aug)
- If municipal source only: golf course draws ~17% of annual supply during peak season overlap
- Mitigation: on-site retention pond could offset 20–40% of irrigation demand
- Recommended: condition approval on irrigation pond construction before opening
